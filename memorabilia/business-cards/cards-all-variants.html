1. Print Specs - What Vendors Need

Card Size3.5" x 2" (89 x 51 mm) - standard business card. Ask vendor if they do 90 x 55 mm (slightly larger, European standard).
Quantity5,200 cards - ask for bulk pricing. Most shops price per 100 or per 1000.
SidesDouble-sided (front + back). Front: main info. Back: branding/tagline.
Resolution300 DPI minimum - vendor will reject anything below 150 DPI. Provide files at 300 DPI or higher.
File FormatPDF (preferred), or high-res PNG/TIFF at 300 DPI. Some vendors accept CorelDRAW (.cdr) or Adobe Illustrator (.ai).
Bleed Area3mm bleed on all sides. Design must extend 3mm beyond the trim line. Total file size with bleed: 95 x 57 mm.
Safe Zone5mm inside from trim edge. No text or logos in this zone - they might get cut off.
Color ModeCMYK for offset printing. RGB is okay for digital printing but colors may shift. Ask the vendor which they prefer.
Paper Stock350 GSM Art Card (standard for business cards). Ask for matte lamination on both sides for the dark design - it prevents fingerprints and looks premium.
Finish Options Option A: Matte lamination both sides (recommended for dark cards)
Option B: Matte lamination + spot UV on the butterfly logo and name (premium, costs more)
Option C: Soft-touch lamination (velvet feel, most premium)
CornersRounded corners (2mm radius) - optional but recommended for modern look. Some vendors charge extra. Standard square corners also fine.
Background#0a0a0a near-black. Tell vendor: "Background is very dark, almost black. Not pure black - it has a slight warmth." For CMYK, use Rich Black: C:60 M:40 Y:40 K:100.
Green Color#4ade80 (bright mint green). In CMYK: approximately C:55 M:0 Y:55 K:0. Ask for a color proof first.

2. What to Say to the Vendor (Script)

Walk into the shop near Munirka Vihar and say:

Opening: "Bhai, mujhe 5,200 business cards chahiye. Double-sided, dark background wale. Timeline ek din ka hai - kab tak ho sakta hai?" Specs batao: "Size standard 3.5 x 2 inch. Paper 350 GSM art card. Dono taraf matte lamination chahiye - dark design hai toh fingerprints na aaye. Rounded corners agar ho sake toh accha hai." Design file ke baare mein: "Main design file PDF mein dunga 300 DPI pe. Bleed 3mm rakha hai. CMYK mein chahiye ya RGB chalega? Agar CMYK chahiye toh bata do, main convert karke dunga." Color proof maango: "Ek green color hai design mein - mint green type. Pehle ek proof print karwa do 5-10 cards ka, toh confirm kar lunga color sahi aa raha hai. Phir bulk run karna." Pricing poochho: "5,200 cards ke liye per card ya per 1000 kya rate hoga? Matte lamination include hai ya alag? Rounded corners ka extra charge?" Spot UV option (agar budget hai): "Ek option aur - butterfly logo aur name pe spot UV ho sakta hai? Uska kya extra charge hoga?" Timeline confirm: "Mujhe jaldi chahiye - 1-2 din mein ho sakta hai kya? Agar aaj design de du toh?"

3. Checklist Before Going to Vendor

  • PDF file of chosen variant(s) at 300 DPI with 3mm bleed - export from a design tool like Canva/Figma/Illustrator
  • Phone mein screenshot dikhao as color reference - vendor ka monitor calibrated nahi hota
  • Decide: matte lamination (budget) or spot UV + matte (premium)
  • Decide: rounded corners ya square
  • Proof print maango - 5-10 cards pehle dekho before bulk
  • Budget: expect Rs 1.5-3 per card for basic, Rs 3-5 per card with spot UV. Total: roughly Rs 8,000-25,000 for 5,200 cards depending on finish.
  • Ask for delivery in 2 batches if urgent - 1,000 first, 4,200 later
