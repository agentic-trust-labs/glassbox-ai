🔧 Fix Plan — 6 Root Causes → 6 Fixes

Fix 1: Strikethrough (3.2→8) — BIGGEST GAP

  • Before: 0.42rem, bright red #dc2626 — too small, too alarming
  • After: 0.6rem, dull gray #a1a1aa text, dark-red strikethrough line #b91c1c, 2px thick
  • Effect: Big, readable, but subdued — the line does the work, not the color

Fix 2: Structure (5.2→8) — Add Glass Panels + Zones

  • Before: Vertical text stack, no visual zones, content floats in center
  • After: Each card has a .gp (glass panel) overlay creating visible structure — header/body/footer zones with separators
  • Each variant gets a unique structural approach (panel, split, bands, frame, accent bar)

Fix 3: Full Card Usage (4.8→8) — Fill All Space

  • Before: Content in center, edges have weak circles
  • After: Glass panels stretch full card, content fills height. Structural elements (bars, bands, frames) use edges

Fix 4: Trust/Transparency Feel (4.5→8)

  • Before: Only butterfly suggests glass
  • After: Glass panel overlays, layered transparency, visible structure = "glass box you can see through"

Fix 5: Personal Side (4.0→8) — 3 Templates

  • Template A (V01,V04,V07,V09): Left-aligned, QR bottom-right, glass panel
  • Template B (V02,V05,V08,V10): Centered, QR bottom, glass panel
  • Template C (V03,V06): Two-zone split: info top, contacts bottom

Fix 6: Uniqueness (4.5→8) — 10 Different Structures

  • V01: Glass panel centered | V02: Dark + glass window | V03: Three green bands
  • V04: Left accent bar + white text | V05: Floating inner card | V06: Diagonal accent + white text
  • V07: Header band + body | V08: Glass frame border | V09: Split panels | V10: Central glass focus
